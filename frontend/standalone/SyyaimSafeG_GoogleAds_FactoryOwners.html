Bidding Strategy
Month 1: Maximize Clicks, ₹600–1,000/day. Month 2+: switch to Target CPA once you hit 30 conversions. Factory owner keywords are competitive — expect ₹120–220 CPC.
📍
Geo Priority
Tier 1: Pune, Chennai, Ahmedabad, Gurugram, Bengaluru (highest factory density + digital adoption). +20% bid modifier. Tier 2: Surat, Ludhiana, Coimbatore.
🕐
Ad Schedule
Mon–Fri 9am–7pm IST at full bid. Factory owners often research after hours too — try 7pm–10pm +10% bid. Reduce weekends by 30%. Desktop +25% device bid.
👤
Audience Layering
Add: "Business Owners" in-market + "Manufacturing Equipment" + "Industrial & Commercial" segments. Layer job title targeting on Display: MD, CEO, Plant Head, Operations Director.
📊
Conversion Tracking
Primary: Demo form submit (value: ₹60,000). Secondary: ROI calculator used, pricing page 30s+, video watched 50%. Enable enhanced conversions for better smart bidding.
🔁
Remarketing
Create lists: Pricing page visitors (14-day), ROI calculator users (30-day), Demo page bounced (7-day). Show Variant C ads to these. Use customer match on existing lead list.
